BatiStory.

We at Batifruits have the simple, pure and lovely purpose to make it easy for babies and kids to enjoy fruit every day.

That's why in 2007 our partners met in Mendoza, Argentina to find the best land to plant and harvest the organic certified fruit orchades.

As time went by they continue putting their minds and hearts to develop the best organic certified fruit puree for babies. Following thorouthly all the quality and traceability of the process and products.

Also carefully keeping in mind all the requirements and needs that mums and customers ask for their babies.

After lots of meetings and tests, they finally came to a new healthy product that provides an easy and happy way to include more fruit in babies daily diet in a simple pouch.

We believe in the power of nature to help develop healthy kids, providing nourishing and tasty food.

It is simple, it is ready to eat, it is organic.

You and your baby will love it!
